About Performing

Focus

We are committed to fulfilling each of our students unique needs in order to grow their ability to express themselves authentically through their chosen art form. No two students are alike and we honor this fact by giving our undivided attention to each challenge as they arise.

Community

Borne2Dance is a socially responsible company and our philosophy is compassion towards all. We are a regular presence throughout our community, volunteering our time and resources to support our students, families, and the community at large.

Faculty

The hallmark of our staff is passion for the arts and for the people that make up our family here. All teachers engage in ongoing education, performances, and other creative endeavors to stay inspired and help better serve students. Our staff is friendly, helpful, reliable, and expertly trained.
